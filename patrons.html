Prof. Aditya Trivedi is a Professor in the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Department at ABV Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management, Gwalior, India. He has about 20 years of teaching experience. Before joining ABV-IIITM in December 2006, he was a Reader in the Deptt. of Electronics and CSE at M.I.T.S., Gwalior. He received his bachelor degree in Electronics Engg. from the Jiwaji University.He did his M.Tech. (Communication Systems) from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kanpur.


 

Prof. S. G. Deshmukh
Director, ABV-IIITM Gwalior, Officiating Director, PDPM IIITDM Jabalpur

Dr. S. R. Pandian is Coordinator of TLC, and Dean (Planning) at IIITDM Kancheepuram. He received his BE in Electronics & Communication Engineering from Madurai-Kamaraj University, and MTech and PhD in Electrical Engineering from IIT Kanpur and IIT Delhi respectively. He is a Senior Member of IEEE. During 1992-2012, he taught and did research at leading universities in Japan and USA. He has over 130 publications in peer-reviewed journals and conferences.His play power invention was selected by the New York Times in 2003 as among the Ideas of the year. His inventions have been featured on the Discovery Channel, Canada and SBS TV korea, as well as national and other international news media.


 

Prof. Banshidhar Majhi
