Note :


  • The registration fee will include Registration kit, soft Conference Proceedings, Networking tea and Working lunch, Conference banquet dinner, Sangam Visit, and City tour.
  • The registration fee does not include accommodation; however, the candidates can be allowed to stay in Visitor Hostels and Hostels on payment basis.
  • If the final manuscript in IEEE format exceeds 6 pages, INR 1000 will be charged per extra page.
  • An additional amount of 18% is to be added for GST.
  • In case of students, valid proof is needed at the registration counter at the time of conference.
  • If the author is registered as the paper presenter in the conference, he/she can present another paper by him/her at the reduced fee @50% of the original registration fee, i.e. the total registration fee will be 1.5x of the cost of registration of 1 paper. This discount is applicable only for one additional paper to be presented by the same author. Basically a candidate is charged according to (1+0.5n)X where n is the number of papers submitted by him/her and X is the respective category registration fee.
  • All heads are non-refundable however, organizing chairs reserves the right to provide some concession subject to certain constraints.
