IEEE CICT Conference

The Conference on Information and Communication Technology (CICT) is an International conference started by the five MHRD funded IIITs (IIIT Allahabad, ABV- IIITM Gwalior, PDPM-IIITDM Jabalpur, IIITDM Kancheepuram, and IIITDM Kurnool) with an aim to provide a forum for researchers, industry professionals, and academicians to present and exchange their ideas in latest technological advancements and innovations in the field of ICT. The CICT will be jointly organized by the five IIITs annually on rotation basis. The CICT 2018 was hosted by PDPM-IIITDM Jabalpur. The third edition of the conference, CICT 2019 will be hosted by IIIT Allahabad, India during December 06-09, 2019. The conference will feature technical paper sessions, poster sessions, and Ph.D. symposium.
